Privacy Policy

At Dark Blend by GBG, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, shipping address, and payment details when you make a purchase.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device used to access our website.
  • Behavioral Information: Pages visited, time spent on our website, and purchase history.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process orders and manage your account.
  • Improve our products and services based on your feedback and behavior.
  • Send promotional offers and updates (you can opt out at any time).
  • Ensure website security and troubleshoot technical issues.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your data with:

  • Service providers (e.g., payment processors, delivery companies) to complete transactions.
  • Legal authorities when required to comply with laws or legal processes.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access to your data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal obligations).
  • Opt out of marketing communications.

6. Cookies

We use cookies to improve user experience and analyze website traffic. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Please review it regularly to stay informed of any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, feel free to contact us at:

Email: privacy@darkblend.com
